#### About

Self-taught repair technician and computer science student at [Rafik Hariri University](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/). Since 2019, I've diagnosed hardware at the component level — traced to the root fault, not swapped at the board.

At [**The Logiq Lab**](https://thelogiqlab.com), every device is handled personally: bench diagnostics, honest repair, and a 60-day warranty. No fix, no fee.

When I'm not at the bench, I'm building software — our site, admin tools, and side projects that sit between circuits and code.
