## About me

I'm a **self-taught electronics repair technician** and **computer science student** at [Rafik Hariri University](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/). Since **2019**, I've been diagnosing and fixing hardware at the **component level** — GPUs, motherboards, laptops, ECUs, and storage — not just swapping boards.

I run [**The Logiq Lab**](https://thelogiqlab.com), my repair shop in Rmeileh, Lebanon. Every device is handled personally: traced to the root fault with proper bench equipment, repaired honestly, and backed by a **60-day warranty**. No fix, no fee.

When I'm not at the bench, I'm building software — this site, our admin tools, and side projects that mix hardware and code.
